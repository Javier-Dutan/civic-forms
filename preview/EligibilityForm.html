Basic Requirements

Please confirm you meet the following requirements before starting your application.

Privacy Act Notice: This information is collected to determine your eligibility for USDS positions. Responses are voluntary, but incomplete information may delay processing of your application.

Question 1 of 6

Are you a United States citizen?

U.S. citizenship is required for all USDS positions due to access to sensitive federal systems and information.

5 U.S.C. § 3301

Question 2 of 6

Are you able to work in Washington, D.C.?

USDS is headquartered in the Executive Office of the President. While hybrid arrangements may be available, regular on-site presence in D.C. is expected.

Question 3 of 6

Are you comfortable with federal government compensation?

USDS positions are typically GS-13 through GS-15, with salaries ranging from approximately $120,000 to $195,000 based on experience and locality.

OPM General Schedule Pay Tables

Question 4 of 6

Can you complete a federal background investigation?

All federal employees undergo a background check that reviews employment history, education, criminal records, credit history, and references.

5 CFR § 731

Question 5 of 6

Can you pass a pre-employment drug test?

The federal government maintains a drug-free workplace. Pre-employment testing is required. This includes marijuana regardless of state laws.

Executive Order 12564

Question 6 of 6

Are you interested in a term of service of at least one year?

USDS tours are typically 1-2 years, with extensions possible up to 4 years. Team members have the greatest impact with a commitment of 12 months or more.
